FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does production take? +

Production time varies by product and order size. We typically require 3–7 business days. For large orders or peak periods (Eid, December), please order well in advance.

Do you offer nationwide delivery? +

Yes! We deliver across South Africa via courier. Delivery costs will be quoted based on your location. Collection is also available.

What is the minimum order quantity? +

Minimum order quantities vary by product. Stickers typically require a minimum of 20 units. Single items are available for products like compact mirrors and jewellery cases. Contact us to confirm.

Can I see a proof before production? +

Absolutely! We always send a digital proof for your approval before going to print. Your satisfaction is our priority.
