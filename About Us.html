ABOUT US

Haqeebaty is a company that was founded by a very dedicated creative lady. She wanted to provide content for her daughters and other muslim children out there that would reinforce Islamic teachings. She wanted top contribute to filling the gap in islamic media and also focus on the minority groups in general

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to provide content that teaches authentic Islamic knowledge to kids. We also aim to provide islamic themed illustrated designs for Islamic homes, to make muslims feel more included in society as these types of designs are currently not in abundance. The ultimate goal is to be a one-stop online shop for islamic themed products.
