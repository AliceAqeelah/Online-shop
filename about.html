Who We Are

Hello, I'm Aneesa Narker

QT Designs is a proudly South African custom gifting business, founded and run by Aneesa Narker. What started as a passion project has grown into a beloved gifting brand trusted by hundreds of families across South Africa.

Everything we create is made with care and intention. Whether you need a beautifully designed chocolate bar for a matric farewell, a personalised compact mirror with an Islamic dua, or custom stickers for a birthday party — we bring your vision to life.

As a Muslim woman and business owner, Islamic values are at the heart of everything QT Designs creates. We aim to fill a gap in the market for high-quality, faith-inspired gifting that feels personal and meaningful.
